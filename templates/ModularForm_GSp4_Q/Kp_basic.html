Dimension formulas for paramodular cusp forms for prime and for weights 3 and higher were proven by Ibukiyama (Dimension formulas of Siegel modular forms of weight 3 and supersingular abelian surfaces, Siegel Modular Forms and Abelian Varieties, Proceedings of the 4-th Spring Conference on Modular Forms and Related Topics, 2007).

The dimensions of weight 2 paramodular cusp forms for primes (with the exceptions of 349, 353, 389, 461, 523, 587) are proven by C. Poor and D. S. Yuen (Paramodular cusp form, arXiv:1004.4699). Poor and Yuen also proves that the only possible weight 2 nonlifts in this range of primes () can only occur at primes 277, 349, 353, 389, 461, 523, 587. The nonlift weight 2 eigenform at is proven; the others are conjectured.
