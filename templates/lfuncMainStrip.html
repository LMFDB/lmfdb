On these pages you can find various L-function information including the functional equation, Dirichlet series, and Euler product, as well as links to the L-function's underlying objects. Also, special values, zeroes, and a plot are available.

First, choose the degree that you want to explore:

Degree 1
Riemann zeta function: $\zeta(s)$

Dirichlet L-function: $L(s,\chi)$
Degree 2
$L(s,f)$: the standard L-function of a holomorphic cusp form

$L(s,f)$: the standard L-function of a Maass form on GL(2)
Degree 3
$L(s, f, \mathrm{sym}^2)$: the symmetric square L-function of a holomorphic cusp form

$L(s, f, \mathrm{sym}^2)$: the symmetric square L-function of a Maass form on GL(2)

$L(s, f)$: the standard L-function of a Maass form on GL(3)
Degree 4
$L(s, f, \mathrm{sym}^3)$: the symmetric cube L-function of a holomorphic cusp form

$L(s, f, \mathrm{sym}^3)$: the symmetric cube L-function of a Maass form on GL(2)

$L(s, F, \mathrm{spin})$: spinor L-function of a Siegel modular form on GSp(4) (genus 2)

$L(s,f)$: standard L-function of a Maass form on the Picard group SL(2,Z[i])

$L(s,f)$: standard L-function of a Maass form on Sp(4)

$L(s,f)$: standard L-function of a Maass form on GL(4)
Degree 5
$L(s, f, \mathrm{sym}^4)$: the symmetric fourth-power L-function of a holomorphic cusp form

$L(s, f, \mathrm{sym}^4)$: the symmetric fourth-power L-function of a Maass form on GL(2)

$L(s, F, \mathrm{stan})$: standard L-function of a Siegel modular form on GSp(4) (genus 2)
