  • I. Miyawaki: Numerical examples of Siegel cusp forms of degree 3 and their zeta-functions, Mem. Fac. Sci. Kyushu Univ. Ser. A 46 (1992), 307--339, MR1195472

S Tsuyumine gives 34 generators for the ring of Siegel modular forms of degree 3 with with respect to the full modular group.. Tsuyumine also gives a formula for the dimensions of the spaces at each weight.

Miyawaki computed enough coefficeints in weight 12 and 14 to determine the the Euler factor at the prime 2, and conjectured the Miyawaki lifts .

C. Poor and D. S. Yuen computed coefficients coefficients in weights 16, 18, 20 and determined the eigenforms.
