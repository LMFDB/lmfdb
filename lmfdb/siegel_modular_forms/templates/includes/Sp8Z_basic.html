The ring of Siegel modular forms of degree 4 with respect to the full modular group

  • C. Poor and D. S. Yuen: Computations of spaces of Siegel modular cusp forms, J. Math. Soc. Japan 59 1 (2007), 185-222, MR2302669

The dimensions for degree 4 Siegel modular cusp forms for the full group for weights were proven by C. Poor and D. S. Yuen (Computations of spaces of Siegel modular cusp forms, J. Math. Soc. Japan 59 1 (2007), 185-222, MR2302669). Poor and Yuen also computed Fourier coefficients and some eigenvalues.

The cusp forms in weights up through 14 are either Duke-Imamoglu-Ikeda lifts or Miyawaki lifts. In weight 16, in addition to these two types of lifts, there are other eigenforms which currently are unknown whether they are some new type of lifts or not. The ring structure is unknown.
