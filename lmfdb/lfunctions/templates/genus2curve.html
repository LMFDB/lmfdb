The L-function $L(s,A)= \sum a_n n^{-s}$ of an abelian surface of {{ KNOWL('genus2.conductor', title='conductor')}} $N$ has an {{ KNOWL('lfunction.euler_product', title='Euler product')}} of the form \[ L(s,E)=\prod_p\prod_{j=1}^4\left(1-\alpha_{j,p} p^{-s}\right)^{-1} \] and satisfies the {{ KNOWL('lfunction.functional_equation', title='functional equation')}} \[ \Lambda(s,A)= N^{s/2}\Gamma_{\mathbb C}(s+1/2)^2\cdot L(s,A)= \varepsilon\Lambda(1-s,A), \] where the sign \(\varepsilon\) is equal to either \(+1\) or \(-1\).
