There exist {{ KNOWL("mf.maass.mwf",title="weight 0 Maass cusp forms")}} on Hecke congruence groups $\Gamma_0(N)$ with even character $\chi$. The L-function \(L(s,f) = \sum a_n n^{-s} \) associated to the Maass cusp form $f$ has an {{ KNOWL('lfunction.euler_product', title='Euler product')}} of the form \[ L(s,f)= \prod_{p\mid N} \left(1 - a_p p^{-s} \right)^{-1}\prod_{p\nmid N} \left(1 - a_p p^{-s} + \chi(p)p^{-2s} \right)^{-1} \] and satisfies a {{ KNOWL("lfunction.functional_equation",title="functional equation")}} of the form \begin{equation} \Lambda(s,f) = N^{s/2} \Gamma_{\R} \left(s + \delta + iR \right) \Gamma_{\R} \left(s + \delta - iR \right) \cdot L(s, f) = \varepsilon \Lambda(1-s,f), \end{equation} where $N$ is the {{ KNOWL("mf.maass.mwf.level",title="level")}}, $R$ the {{ KNOWL("mf.maass.mwf.eigenvalue",title="eigenvalue")}} of the Maass cusp form, $\varepsilon$ is the {{ KNOWL('lfunction.sign', title='sign')}} and $\delta$ is 1 (or 0) when $f$ is odd (or even).

The eigenvalue is labelled with an 'e' or an 'o' depending on whether the corresponding Maass cusp form is even or odd.
