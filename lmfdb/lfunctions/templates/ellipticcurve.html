The L-function $L(s,E)= \sum a_n n^{-s}$ of an elliptic curve of {{ KNOWL('ec.q.conductor', title='conductor')}} $N$ has an {{ KNOWL('lfunction.euler_product', title='Euler product')}} of the form \[ L(s,E)=\prod_{p\mid N} \left(1 - a_p p^{-s} \right)^{-1}\prod_{p\nmid N} \left(1 - a_p p^{-s} + p^{-2s} \right)^{-1} \] and satisfies the {{ KNOWL('lfunction.functional_equation', title='functional equation')}} \[ \Lambda(s,E)= N^{s/2}\Gamma_{\mathbb C}(s+1/2)\cdot L(s,E)= \varepsilon\Lambda(1-s,E), \] where the sign \(\varepsilon\) is equal to either \(+1\) or \(-1\).
