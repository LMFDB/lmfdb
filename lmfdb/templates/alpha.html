Welcome to the alpha version of the LMFDB, the database of L-functions, modular forms, and related objects. This site hosts pre-release sections of the LMFDB that are not yet ready for the beta or production sites.

These sections, linked on the sidebar to the left, are still under development and should not be considered reliable enough for citation in a research publication. In particular

  • object labels may change,
  • there may be errors, both mathematical issues in the data and in the user interface,
  • data may not yet be computed to a desired level of completeness,
  • the user interface may still be in progress.

Each section specifies the reasons it remains an alpha version.

That being said, the LMFDB Collaboration hopes that you find this preliminary data helpful, and you are welcome to contact us with suggestions, errors, or to get involved.
