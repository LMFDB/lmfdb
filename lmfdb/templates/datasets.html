If you have a dataset you would like to contribute to the LMFDB, please contact a member of the Editorial Board. In order to be considered for inclusion, your dataset should

  1. fit within the LMFDB's general scope (L-functions, modular forms and related objects),
  2. have a publicly available description of how it was computed, either through a paper or a code repository,
  3. be available under the CC-BY-SA license.
